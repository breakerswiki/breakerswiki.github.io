Tia Langray

History

Tia Langray, a Thai kickboxer, embarks on a quest to find her brother, unaware that he encountered Bai-Hu and met a tragic end. Sho, a character in the game, reminds her of her brother, a fact she acknowledges.

Gameplay

Tia is the fastest character, with strong pressure, mobility and punish options. Mastering her allows you to dictate the game's pace in most matchups.

Attack

Tia's dash pressure is key to her gameplay, boosting mobility to create openings and build meter.

short dash A

Though not easy to execute, if done correctly, this pressure keeps the opponent blocking, builds your meter, and pushes them toward the corner.

short dash 5C and 5D

Dash far 5D and 5C are safe on block and serve as anti-air. They’re hard to react to, so opponents must use preemptive crouching light attacks to counter.

Rapid charge

Her fastest super, it comes out quickly and punishes projectiles and most unsafe moves. Easily connects after anti-air normals.

Anti-air

623P

623A comes fast, letting her pass through projectiles. If it hits as an anti-air you can follow up with Super. 623C hits higher and has more recovery; both have startup invulnerability.

far 5C and 5D

Dash far 5D and 5C are safe on block and serve as anti-air. Confirm 5D with super 236236P and 5C with Soul Spike (214K) for a super juggle.

5A

Super/Special cancelable. Use it to hit opponents jumping close to you and counter ambiguous jump-ins and cross-ups. 5A anti-air can lead to a juggle combo.

Burning Revolver

an effective way to punish jumps and cross-up attempts
