Tia Langray

History

Tia Langray, a Thai kickboxer, embarks on a quest to find her brother, unaware that he encountered Bai-Hu and met a tragic end. Sho, a character in the game, reminds her of her brother, a fact she acknowledges.

Gameplay

Tia is a fast fighter that can quickly switch directions on opponents to slip through their defense. Her dash jab pressure is very strong and she has excellent conversions off of stray hits. Opponents will be forced to use a breaker to get out of her pressure or risk being put in the corner. Using Rapid charge (Super) she can be a threat from anywhere on the screen. She also has a good fireball and poke game.

Anti-air

anti-air with 623P or st.HK
both of which can be followed by super (Rapid charge)
Burning Revolver is also an effective way to punish jumps and cross-up attempts.

Super usage

Rapid charge super is used as a combo ender and to punish opponent's unsafe moves and projectiles effectively

Burning Revolver can be utilized as a combo finisher, usable both in the air and on the ground. Thanks to its invulnerability during startup, it functions as a baiting tool for reversals or frametraps. Upon hitting, it directs opponents towards the corner.

High/low mixup

  1. j.HP > air soul spike or j.HP > cr.LK
  2. fuzzy: j.HP > 8 HP > Super Burning Revolver

Fuzzy

  • j.LK > j.8HP > Super Burning Revolver

Dash pressure

short dash jab

If executed correctly, this pressure leaves no gap and allows you to maintain the advantage while also charging your super bar.

dash far st.HK

far st.HK is safe on block and doubles as an anti-air. Hit airborne opponents for a super juggle.

dash st.HP

far st.HP is safe on block. Hit airborne opponents and confirm it with Soul Spike (214K) for a super juggle

Saizo

Rasen Kyaku
Overhead, negative on hit and block with a long recovery. Leaves ample time for punishment. Saizo rarely resorts to this move unless finishing off his opponent

Hiryuu HP
A bomb that is often used to cancel unsafe moves (for example, slide > bomb is safe in most matchups). At close range you can punish with cr.LP or st.LP > Super (236236P)

Yashagami
Yashagami HP knocks down opponents, while Yashagami LP serves as a reversal and anti-air. This move must be canceled into a super due to its negative frames on hit and block. Be cautious on block; Saizo still has a few frames to execute a super, causing hesitation in your usual punishment. In such instances, your Super (236236P) ensures a guaranteed punish

st.HP
Close st.HP hits low, while the far version has significant recovery, making it punishable even on hit.

Sho

Onigawara
An overhead, safe on block, and cancelable from all of his close normals, making it a strong move. However, it's punishable during its startup frames. It's also breakable when canceled into 236HK.

Tobihizageri
A pressing tool, safe on block, but depending on the range, Sho might end up within your throw range or leave an opening in the knee animation where you can react with 623P

Sashikomi Kick
This divekick is challenging to handle because it can be executed from any jump height. Additionally, it is super-cancelable on hit, block, or whiff.
At high height and when Sho has no meter, you can reliably counter it with your anti-air (st.HK, st.LP, 623P). It is also breakable with 623P, backdash, or throw (though throw can be countered by sho's 214HK)

Rekkou Ha
A basic fireball that can be punished when blocked at point-blank range. Tia's Super 236236P goes through fireballs and can punish blockstrings with small or no pushback. (example: on corner cr.LK > cr.HK > 236P)

Ranki Ryuu Geri
Ranki Ryuu Geri is tricky as it can be followed by air super (Geki Rekkou Ha). However, it's still minus on block. If you have meter, a forward j.HP > Burning revolver Super (214214P) guarantees a punish.

Geki Rekkou Ha
Break with a backdash if used at a high height. For example: divekick x super or fuzzy j.HK x super.

Tia

Soul Spike

Ground Soul Spike LK/HK are not overhead, block low.
Soul Spike LK has pushback and minimal recovery. you can punish it with super (236236P)
Soul Spike HK has no pushback, making it easier to punish. Punish with st.HP or a Throw. Before attempting a block punish, check her super meter first. Tia can counter with Burning Revolver. A throw is a guaranteed punish if she uses Soul Spike HK.

Rapid Gale
