Tia Langray

Backstory

Tia Langray, a Thai kickboxer, embarks on a quest to find her brother, unaware that he encountered Bai-Hu and met a tragic end. Sho, a character in the game, reminds her of her brother, a fact she acknowledges.

Overview

Tia is a fast fighter that can quickly switch directions on opponents to slip through their defense. Her dash jab pressure is very strong and she has excellent conversions off of stray hits. Opponents will be forced to use a breaker to get out of her pressure or risk being put in the corner. Using Rapid charge (Super) she can be a threat from anywhere on the screen. She also has a good fireball and poke game.

Basic knowledge

Quick moves and meter build

Her overall speed can dictate the pace/rhythm of the match. Mastering her dash and fast special moves allows you to build meter quickly.

Anti-air

anti-air with 623P or st.HK
both of which can be followed by super (Rapid charge)
Burning Revolver is also an effective way to punish jumps and cross-up attempts.

Super usage

Rapid charge
  • combo ender
  • punish unsafe moves and projectiles
Burning Revolver
  • Combo ender
  • Can be done in the air
  • Anti-air
  • Useful for getting your opponent the closest to the corner
  • Startup invulnerability
  • Can be used to bait your opponent: use it as a reversal, frametrap or with empty jump > super

High/low mixup

  1. j.HP > air soul spike (overhead) or j.HP > cr.LK
  2. fuzzy: j.HP > 8 HP > Super Burning Revolver

Dash pressure

short dash jab

If executed correctly, this pressure leaves no gap and allows you to maintain the advantage while also charging your super bar.

dash far st.HK

far st.HK is safe on block and doubles as an anti-air. Hit airborne opponents for a super juggle.

dash st.HP

far st.HP is safe on block. Hit airborne opponents and confirm it with Soul Spike (214K) for a super juggle

Fuzzy

j.LK > j.8HP > Super Burning Revolver
