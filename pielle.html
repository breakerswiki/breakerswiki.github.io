Pielle Montatio

History

Pielle is an Italian nobleman and fencer. With his sword he can harness the power of lightning.

Pielle's alter-ego, George, is a Frenchman who was robbed in Italy and has hated Italians ever since.

Gameplay

Pielle is challenging to play because of his slow special recovery and lack of invincible reversals without supers. This character requires solid fundamentals and the ability to read your opponent while taking risks.

Neutral

Pielle struggles with zoning. His fireballs have high recovery and are unsafe on block which makes his pressure (e.g., cr.LPx2 > 236LP) unreliable.

His best options are at close range, where he can poke with st.LP or cr.LP, both of which can be converted into 214P, overhead LK+HK, or super (236236P).

Anti-air

Anti-air options

  • Thunder Super (214214P)
  • close st.HP (effective to counter crossups)
  • slide (cr.HK) useful in against backjumps on landing
  • 214214K can punish jumps from long range

His anti-air special, 623P, has low priority and can leave you vulnerable even on hit. Instead, use his super (214214P) with invulnerable startup, or rely on normals like close j.HP, st.HP, or far st.HK.

⚠️ If 623LP hits a grounded opponent, you get long recovery and no knockdown, so stick to using it as an anti-air.

Using his slide as an anti-air allows for OTG setups into Super 236236P or 214214P (corner).

Super usage

Robin Super (236236P) goes through projectiles, in some matchups you can punish your opponent projectiles from far away

His thunder super (214214P) is an effective anti-air and his rose super (214214K) can whiff punish or anti-air from max range

Overhead LK+HK

You can cancel most normals into an overhead. On hit, confirm with LP or st.LK, and if the opponent is standing, follow up with Rose Train.

⚠️ Be careful when using it ! Cancelling any normal into an overhead does not combo, instead, it creates a gap and leaves you vulnerable. Overhead confirms are also breakable.

  • LK+HK ❌ st.LK > 623HP
  • LK+HK ❌ st.LK > 214P.P.P
  • LK+HK ❌ cr.LK > 214HP.HP.HP
  • LK+HK ❌ cr.LK > 623HP
  • LK+HK ❌ st.LPx2 > 214LK (Only on standing opponents)
  • LK+HK ❌ st.LPx2 > 214LP.LP.LP

if you perform an overhead while close to a crouching opponent, it will whiff, and you will pass behind them.
