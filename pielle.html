Pielle Montatio

History

Pielle is an Italian nobleman and fencer. With his word he can harness the power of lightning. Pielle's alter-ego is known as George.

George is a Frenchman who was once robbed during a trip to Italy and has harbored a hatred for Italians ever since.

Gameplay

Pielle, a classic zoning fighter, has flaws in his moveset like slow recovery after specials and no invincible reversal unless using supers. Play him by taking risks and making the most of situational supers.

Neutral

Pielle isn't great at zoning with projectiles. They don't work well against someone who knows the matchup. His Fireball have a lot of recovery, the opponent easily has time to jump and punish you.

It's also unsafe on guard: Most of his pressure with fireball (e.g., cr.LP > 236LP) are not safe on block. There's also a gap before the fireball hits the opponent.

Anti-air

  • 623LP/HP
  • Thunder Super (214214P)
  • close st.HP (effective to counter crossups)
  • slide (cr.HK) useful in against backjumps on landing

His slide allows for OTG (Off The Ground) setups into Super Swords/Thunder.

⚠️ Avoid using 623LP in combos; it results in a quick recovery and no knockdown for the opponent, making you susceptible to easy punishment. Try to only Use 623LP as an anti-air.

Overhead LK+HK

you can combo overhead with LK, LP, close st.HP or cr.HK.
midscreen: If you perform an overhead while close to a crouching opponent, it will whiff, and you will pass behind them. ⚠️ On guard, every normal canceled into an overhead leaves a gap.

Side switch

  • close st.HK > 214HK
  • j.HK > cr.HP > 214HK

Fuzzy & instant overhead

instant overhead with neutral j.LK
fuzzy:

  • j.HP > j.LK
  • j.HP > j.HP

Meterless combos

  • cr.LK > 214HP.HP.HP
  • LK+HK > cr.LK > 214HP.HP.HP
  • LK+HK > cr.LK > 623HP
  • LK+HK > st.LPx2 > 214LK (Standing hit only)
  • LK+HK > st.LPx2 > 214LP.LP.LP
  • cr.LPx3 > 214HP.HP.HP
  • j.HK > cr.HP > 214HK
  • j.HK > cr.HP > 214HP.HP.HP

Metered combos

  • cr.LP x 2 > Super Swords (strict timing)
  • j.HK > close st.HK > Super Swords
  • j.HK > cr.HP > Super Swords
  • corner: j.HK > cr.HP > LP+HP > Super Swords / Thunder

Anti-air

  • close st.HK > Super Swords / Thunder
  • close st.HP > Super Swords
  • close st.HP > Super Thunder (corner)
  • cr.HP > Super Flower
  • cr.HP > Super thunder (corner or close range)
  • corner: cr.HP > LP+HP > Super Swords / Thunder (1st hit must be close to the ground)
  • far. st.HK > Swords
  • slide > Super Swords / Thunder (OTG)

Air-to-air

  • j.LK/HK/HP/LP > 623LP (1st hit must be close to the ground)
