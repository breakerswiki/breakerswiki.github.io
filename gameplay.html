How to play

Button layout

The KOF (King Of Fighters) layout, standard in arcades, is user-friendly, allowing easy execution of complex moves.

Throw

Press + or +

Throws beats meaty normals
Some characters have additional throws inputs or air throws.

Tech rolling

On wake-up, press 3 buttons
ex: ++

Tech roll give you more options to escape from your opponent's pressure after a knockdown.
It only works after after a non-super hit
Tech rolls can also be delayed

Dashes

or

Backdashes and dashes build meter. All characters can perform dashes except Condor. Depending on the character, Certain backdashes are invincible.

Some characters can execute short dashes by interrupting them with a crouching attack or guard. Additionally, some dashes can be canceled into a jump, providing :

  • a longer forward jump
  • a shorter back jump
  • a slightly longer neutral jump

    • Note: A slightly longer neutral jump can sets up ambiguous attacks on your opponent's wakeup.

    Dizzy

    To recover from a dizzy, mash buttons and directions.

    You can be dizzied after repeated hits.
    Your character will be invulnerable while he is recovering.
    If you mash directions and buttons quickly enough, you will skip the dizzy state and start recovering immediately.

    Chip damage

    Chip damage refers to damage taken when blocking an attack
    It can kill your opponent if they are low enough on health.

    Note: Super moves will only deal a small amount of chip damage. To kill your opponent, special moves are a better choice.

    Breakers

    Breaker is a defensive mechanic that allows you to break out of specific combos. This can be achieved using certain specials, normals, backdashes, throws, or supers

    Most character have breakable and unbreakable combos. Check character guide for more infos !

    Master unbreakable combos for guaranteed damage and practice recognizing breakable combos from your opponent.
    In high-level matches, this mechanic is often used as mind games (ex: bait defenders into reversals)

    Risk system

    The Risk System increases the damage of your next attack over time when you are not attacking or defending.
    The damage cap is different for each character.

    Once the damage cap is reached (around 30 sec in-game) your next hit will likely be about double.

    The winner of a round has less damage resistance in the next round, while the loser has slightly increased defense.
    When there is a significant difference in health, the player with less health is immune to damage mod until both players have similar health.
