Button layout

The KOF (King Of Fighters) layout, standard in arcades, is user-friendly, allowing easy execution of complex moves.

Throw

Press + or +

Throws beats meaty normals. Some characters have additional throws inputs or air throws.

Tech rolling

On wake-up, press 3 buttons
ex: ++

Tech roll give you more options to escape from your opponent's pressure after a knockdown. It only works after after a non-super hit. Tech rolls can also be delayed

Dashes

or

Backdashes and dashes fill your meter. All can dash except Condor. Certain characters backdashes give invincibility frames. Some can execute short dashes by canceling with a crouching attack, guard, or jump, granting a longer forward jump ; a shorter back jump ; a slightly longer neutral jump.

A dash canceled by a neutral jump can set up ambiguous attacks on your opponent's wakeup.

Dizzy

To recover from a dizzy, mash buttons and directions.

You can be dizzied after repeated hits. Your character will be invulnerable while he is recovering. If you mash directions and buttons quickly enough, you will skip the dizzy state and start recovering immediately.

Chip damage

Chip damage is the damage incurred when blocking an attack. It can secure a win if your opponent's health is low enough. Note that super moves only inflict minimal chip damage. To finish off your opponent, special moves are more effective.

Breakers

Breaker is a defensive mechanic that allows you to break out of specific combos. This can be achieved using certain specials, normals, backdashes, throws, or supers

Master unbreakable combos for guaranteed damage and practice recognizing breakable combos from your opponent. In high-level matches, this mechanic is often used as mind games (ex: bait defenders into reversals)

Risk system

The Risk System increases the damage of your next attack over time when you are not attacking or defending.

The damage cap is different for each character. Once the damage cap is reached (around 30 sec in-game) your next hit will likely be about double.

The winner of a round has less damage resistance in the next round, while the loser has slightly increased defense.
When there is a significant difference in health, the player with less health is immune to damage mod until both players have similar health.
