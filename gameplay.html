This layout is the default and most commonly used, enabling easy execution of multiple button combinations.

Throw

while standing, or+

Throws beats meaty normals. Some characters have additional throws inputs or air throws. Throwing is also an effective way to use the break mechanic.

Supers

The Super Gauge holds up to 3 stocks, when a stock is full, players can use a Super move.

Build super meter by performing normals, specials, dashes, or taunts. Also by getting hit or blocking.

Tech rolling

On wake-up, press 3 buttons. ex: ++

Tech roll give you more options to escape from your opponent's pressure after a knockdown. It only works after after a non-super hit. Tech rolls can also be delayed

Dashes

or

All characters except Condor can dash. Some backdashes offer invincibility. Certain characters can perform quick dashes by canceling them with normals or jumps to adjust jump lengths.

Dizzy

To recover from a dizzy, mash buttons and directions.

Repeated hits can cause dizziness, but mashing directions and buttons skips it, triggering immediate recovery with brief invincibility frames.

Chip damage

Chip damage happens during block and can secure a win when the opponent's health is low. Super moves deal little chip damage, special moves are more effective for finishing off an opponent.

Breakers

Sho performs a breakable combo. Rila counters after Sho's 5C, indicated by the green circle showing a successful Breaker, similar to wakeup reversals.

A Breaker is a defensive tool that lets you escape specific combos. Depending on the character, it can be triggered by specials, normals, backdashes, throws, or supers.

Master unbreakable combos for guaranteed damage, and recognize your opponentâ€™s breakable combos. Use Breakers for mind games, such as baiting defenders into reversals.

Risk system

The Risk System increases the damage of your next attack over time when you are not attacking or defending.

Damage caps vary by character. After about 30 seconds in-game, the next hit can deal double damage.

The round winner has less damage resistance in the next round, while the loser gets a slight defense boost.

When players have significantly different health, the one with less health is immune to damage modification until their health is similar.
