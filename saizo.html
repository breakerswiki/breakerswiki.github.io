Saizo Tobikageno

Backstory

Saizo Tobikageno is a ninja seeking revenge for the slaughter of his clan.

Overview

Saizo is primarily a zoner but he has the ability to switch rapidly between strong zoning and strong offense. The ability to threaten both amplify the effectiveness of each.

Basic knowledge

Offense

his forward j.HP has strong priority over normals and most specials.
Dashing grounded attacks give him the ability to maintain pressure, even from a distance.

safe blockstring:
cr.LP x 2 > st.LK > slide > bomb

Zoning

236 LP (kunai) is a strong fast-moving projectile to chip and keep opponents at distance. It passes through other projectiles (except Saizo).

236 HP (Bombs) are slower, but they recover much faster than other projectiles. They can be used more frequently to keep opponents pressured.

j.HP and neutral j.HP are both effective air-to-air attacks.

Anti-air

623P swords
If it hits a jumping opponent, it can be followed by a super.

close st.HK
can be canceled into super flames or 623P swords

214 LP
a reliable anti-air because of its invincibility frames.

Breaker

214 LP is your breaker move. It has an invincible startup and leads to Hair Super (236236 K)

⚠️ Minus even on hit. This special must be canceled with super.

close. st.HP

close st.HP hits low, you can use it to trick your opponent while he’s standing.
example: (corner) j.HP > st.LP x 2 > st.HP > bomb > Super

close. st.HK

⚠️ There are far and close versions of the standing HK.It is important to know at what range each one triggers as with the long-distance one, you are vulnerable.

⚠️ close st.HKx2 is a fake combo (breakable)

Close st.HK usage

Offense

Strong OS : close st.HK/throw (see Option selects). It’s chainable and cancelable.

On block, close st.HK is positive, you can try to :

  • Continue your pressure (ex: st.HK > cr.LK)
  • Or, wait/delay your following attack to bait a backdash/jump/reversal
Note: close st.HK has a small pushback on block, which keeps you out of the throw range.


Defense

It’s a reliable anti-air, as it comes fast and has a large hitbox.
use it when you don't have time to react with a jump-back HP, hair LP, or sword

As an anti-air, it leads to significant damage due to its special/super cancelable properties.
Try to do 6HK, because there’s a shortcut that makes it easier to convert into swords. HK > 623HP becomes 6HK > 23HP

Chip damage

  • 623HP
  • 236LP
  • 214HP

Kunai (236LP) does more chip than bomb (236HP)

Heavy Hair (214HP) and Sword HP (623HP) deal the most chip damage among all of Saizo's special and super moves.

Pressings

exemples:
cr.LK > close. st.HK slide > bomb

throw/st.HK OS

Coupling Dash Guard with Back Throw (back+HP+HK) makes for an aggressive throw/anti-air option select.

throw OS (4HP+HK) :

  • if your opponent is trying to jump : back+HK (anti-air)
  • if your opponent stays at throw-range : back+HP (throw)

Fuzzy

  • j.HP > j.HK
  • j.HK > j.HK
