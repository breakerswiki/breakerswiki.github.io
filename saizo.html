Saizo Tobikageno

History

Saizo Tobikageno, a ninja driven by the desire for vengeance, pursues justice for the annihilation of his clan.

Gameplay

Saizo is a powerful zoner. His sword moves can easily hit opponents from a distance. He has projectiles that can even pierce through their attacks. His speed and agility allow him to quickly switch between offense and defense.

Offense

j.C
His forward and back j.C has great priority over most normals and some specials. A lot of the cast will have a hard time anti-airing this move

Short dashes
Cancel dashes with cr.A or 2B for dashing crouch attacks, letting you maintain pressure safely and react to potential opponent mistakes, all while closing distance.

5C
close 5C hits low, use it to trick your opponent guard while he’s standing. example: j.C > st.A > 5C > 214P > super

⚠️ Far 5C is highly unsafe on block and hit, so use cautiously to avoid punishment

5D
close 5D is positive, you can try to :

  • Continue your pressure on block (ex: 5D > 2B)
  • Or, wait/delay your following attack to bait a backdash/jump/reversal

close 5D has a small pushback on block, which keeps you out of the throw range.

⚠️ Many players often use the 2-hit combo (5D > 5D), but be careful—it's breakable after the first hit.

⚠️ There are the far and close versions of standing D. Knowing their range is key, as the far one leaves you vulnerable on block or hit.

kunai
Use 236 A (kunai) for quick, strong chip damage and zoning. It cuts through opponent projectiles, except his own.

bombs
236 C (Bombs) are slower than kunai but they recover much faster. They can be used more frequently on close range to keep opponents pressured.

Anti-air

Swords - 623P
The A version is faster, making it better for midrange, while the C version is suited for long-range. when it hits airborne opponent, you can juggle with flames super for more damage.

close 5D :
Can be canceled into super flames or 623P swords. It's a fast, reliable anti-air with a large hitbox, leading to big damage from special/super cancels. Use 6D for easier sword conversions: 5D > 623C becomes 6D > 23C

⚠️ There are the far and close versions of standing D. Knowing their range is key, as the far one leaves you vulnerable on block or hit.

214 A
His best anti-air due to its long invincibility frames. Use it only when you have meter, so you can cancel it into hair super; otherwise, you'll be left at a disadvantage.

When you have meter, 214 A is also your best break move. It’s fast, invincible, and always should be confirmed into Hair Super (236236 K) to avoid disadvantage on block or hit.

Super usage

Musou Shinra
Long-range anti-air super, and extends juggle combos seamlessly from 623P or close 5D

Musou Tenha
Summons a pillar of flames. Can serve as an anti-air, but it's slower than Hair special (214P), which is super cancelable. The A version is fixed directly in front of Saizo, while the C version tracks the opponent.

Senku Ensatsujin
Saizo executes an auto combo with extremely fast startup—possibly the fastest in the game, allowing you to punish many moves. Confirm with Hair (214 A) on hit.

Chip damage

Chip with projectiles, swords, or hair. Heavy Hair (214C) and Sword C (623C) deal the most chip damage among all of Saizo's specials and supers.

Throw ~ 5D OS

Coupling Dash with 5D and Back Throw (back+C+D) makes for an aggressive throw/anti-air option select.

throw OS (4C+D) :

  • if your opponent is trying to jump : back+D (anti-air)
  • if your opponent stays at throw-range : back+C (throw)

Saizo

Rasen Kyaku +

Overhead, unsafe on hit and block, leaves ample time for punishment. Saizo rarely resorts to this move unless finishing off his opponent

Ryuuei Kyaku ++

Slide into bomb is safe unless you have meter; punish with super (236236K). On corner, punish with 2B

Yashagami +

Unsafe on hit, this move must be super-canceled. 214C knocks down opponents, while 214A is fast and serves as an anti-air. Be cautious even on block; Saizo has time to cancel into a super, which may disrupt your usual punishment.

Standing

Close 5C hits low, while the far version has significant recovery, making it punishable even on hit.

Engetsuzan +

punish with dash > 2A

Pielle

Saber Bash +

Despite its animation, this move isn't an overhead. Punish with 2B or 236236K and on whiff with 214A

Flying Peach Bomber +

This one is an overhead, but if done close to you, it will whiff, and he'll pass behind you. When used in a blockstring, there's a gap, no matter which normal move it was canceled from. Punish on whiff with 214A

Bonjour Shot +

A projectile with a very slow recovery. You can whiff punish with 236A At close range, punish on block or hit with 2B or 2C. Mid range with super 236236K.

Robin Liner +

An anti-air special with slow recovery and no reversal properties. On hit, 623A keeps you standing for a punish, while 623C knocks you down.

Monsieur Crash +.

A three-hit move, easily punishable. However, Pielle can delay each hit to counter your punish attempt.

Merci Un Deux Trois +++

An aerial dive that can deceive your anti-air or jump. When you block all 3 hits, Pielle passes behind you. Punish with 5D

Super Rose +

A super that crosses the entire screen with a wide hitbox, useful for countering jump attempts. Whiff punish at super flash with 214A.

Thunder Croiser +

punish with 214C > 236236K

Punish on block with 236236K, but be cautious, Sho can cancel late recovery frames with 214K. You can input 214A during startup. On hit, break with 214A after the 1st hit.
