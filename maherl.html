Sheik Maherl

History

Sheik Maherl found a genie's lamp, making three wishes wisely: becoming a balloon, spitting fire, and enslaving the genie to fight for him. Now, armed only with his lamp and sword, Maherl battles the world's strongest challengers.

Gameplay

Maherl is a slow character focused on poking. His sword special deals strong damage and is usually safe on block. He has tricky command throw setups but lacks special or super confirms from low attacks.

Overview

Maherl can be tricky to play, his slow movement and lack of confirms from low attacks make him challenging, but he can surprise with long-range sword specials. He relies on gimmicks to find openings like Abodement Step and Super Genie, which are safe at the right range.

Swords

214P
214P can be hit confirmed from st.HP, cr.HP, st.HK, and st.LK.

214K
214K works great as an anti-air and juggle confirm up close after st.HK or st.HP.

Anti-air

  • far st.LK
  • 214K
  • close st.HP (anti cross-up)
  • Super genie (214214P)

After using st.LK as an anti-air, you can juggle into a super (214214P).

Supers

Genie Super
Startup invincibility and safe on block

⚠️ There's a gap right before the genie throws his punch. On block, some characters can use their invincible moves to dodge the punch and punish.

Sword Super
Not much invincibility for the whole body, but a bunch against projectiles

Step/Sword glitch

LP+HP > Kara 214P or 214K

1 frame cancel window to get the glitch :

To trigger the glitch, you need to cancel LP+HP with a sword special at the 3rd frame.

  • ✅ Frame 3 of LP+HP = sword glitch
  • ❌ Frame 2 or sooner = sword
  • ❌ Frame 4 or later = LP+HP
Sword glitch variations

HP/HK swords:
Maherl travels to the opposite side of the opponent.

LP/LK swords:
Result in a shorter distance, Maherl stays on the same side.

Body splash:
During the peak of flight, Maherl can perform a cr.HP (body splash) that stays active throughout the entire descent.

If you don't do anything during the glitch, Maherl teleports back to the ground. Seize the opportunity to surprise your opponent with a throw or 360 super.
