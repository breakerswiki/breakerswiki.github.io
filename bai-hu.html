Huang Bai-Hu

History

Bai-Hu is the boss of the game. He started the Breakers Revenge tournament in order to find the most powerful fighters.

Gameplay

Bai-Hu is a secret character. To select Bai-Hu, simultaneously press up on both player 1 and player 2 controls at the character select screen. His moves require button combinations and he has high defense, stun resistance, and an invincible dash.

In tournaments, Bai-Hu's overpowered moveset makes matches unfair and less competitive, leading to his ban to ensure fairness for all players.
