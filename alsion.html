Alsion III

History

Alsion III is an undead ancient Egyptian with rubber-like limbs who uses electric and poison attacks.

Alsion III's alter-ego is known as Atoum. Atoum is a fan of ancient civilization who dresses up like a mummy. When he is in this form, he can use the techniques of the "Pharaoh Taijutsu" style. In reality, he is a descendant of Alsion III.

Gameplay

Alsion III is a zoning character with long-range attacks but very slow movement and no strong defensive tools. He’s tricky to use and needs patience and good game knowledge.

Neutral

2C
2C is low profile. Use it to pass below projectiles.

Poison Fog
Use Poison Fog to protect yourself from most projectiles (except Saizo's kunai). This move also allows you to charge your meter bar.

back jump C
Jump back C can be useful for dealing with jump-in attacks due to its long range.

dash knee
Use Dash Knee (236K) with long-reaching normals to maintain pressure on your opponent. Try sequences like 5A > 236K or 2B > 236K at max range to ensure safety except againsst saizo super.

While airborne, confirm jA with Dash Knee (236B) to hit and push your opponent closer to the corner.

Defense

anti-air
His most reliable anti-air are 5D and B+D (close range).

5D
When 5D hits an airborne opponent, they bounce on the ground, enabling in corner a super juggle with 214124P thanks to its quick startup.

B+D
The first hit of B+D is cancelable. Cancel it with 1236A to maintain pressure or 236B to switch sides when you're in the corner

Offense

This character isn't designed for offense; you often need to take risks and rely on gimmicks to create openings.

Deadly Claw
Deadly Claw (214K) is unsafe but serves as a gimmick, confusing opponents with its landing side and easily comboing into super for extra damage.

Poison Fog
Use Poison Fog to maintain pressure and chip damage after a hard knockdown.

⚠️ Be careful, the C version has a longer recovery. Avoid using it against characters who can evade your projectiles with their invulnerable specials or supers

Breaking

Pharaoh Dance (super 214214P) is Alsion's top breaker move. It has an invulnerable startup, hits fast, and juggles against airborne foes.

Deadly Claw (214K) can also function as a breaker due to its invulnerable startup. However, use it extremely sparingly as it can be confusing and easily punishable.
