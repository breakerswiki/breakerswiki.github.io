Alsion III

History

Alsion III is an undead ancient Egyptian with rubber-like limbs who uses electric and poison attacks.

Alsion III's alter-ego is known as Atoum. Atoum is a fan of ancient civilization who dresses up like a mummy. When he is in this form, he can use the techniques of the "Pharaoh Taijutsu" style. In reality, he is a descendant of Alsion III.

Gameplay

Alsion III is a long-range fighter. He has a ton of long range tools but not the best priority. His fireball isn't suitable for zoning. Once under pressure without meter he has no real breaker, opening him up to combos and strings that are normally breakerable.

Defense

st.HK: If it hits in the air, the opponent bounces on the ground, providing a free super juggle in the corner. Preferably use the super (214124P) which has the fastest startup.

cr.HP is low profile. Use it to pass below projectiles.

Use Poison Fog to protect yourself from most projectiles (except Saizo's kunai). This move also allows you to charge your meter bar.

Jump back HP can be useful for dealing with jump-in attacks due to its long range.

While airborne, use j.LP > Deadly Claw (214K) to push your opponent closer to the corner.

Offense

This character isn't particularly strong on offense; you frequently have to take risks and rely on gimmicks to create openings against your opponent.

Deadly Claw (214K) can be confusing regarding which side you'll land on. You can use it during pressure situations or after resetting an airborne opponent. Additionally, it combos into super easily, making it great for dealing damage.

Use Poison Fog to maintain pressure and chip damage after a hard knockdown. Be careful, the HP version has a longer recovery. Avoid using it against characters who can evade your projectiles with their invulnerable specials

Pressure

Use Dash Knee (236K) with long-reaching normals to maintain pressure on your opponent. Try sequences like st.LP > 236K or cr.LK > 236K at max range to ensure safety.

In the corner, you can bait your opponent into jumping or using a reversal, then punish them with the right setup (See video at the top of the page)

You can also use it in the air to confirm air-to-air normals or punish moves with long recoveries, such as fireballs. Be careful, while it's quite fast, it is vulnerable to whiffed attacks and unsafe if landed at close range

Breaking

Pharaoh Dance (super 214214P) is Alsion's top breaker move. It has an invulnerable startup, hits fast, and juggles against airborne foes.

Deadly Claw (214K) can also function as a breaker due to its invulnerable startup. However, use it extremely sparingly as it can be confusing and easily punishable.
