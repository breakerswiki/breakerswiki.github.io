Sho Kamui

History

Sho Kamui, Breakers Revenge's Karate master and protagonist, joins the FIST Tournament to showcase his martial art's power and confront his old rival, Lee Dao-Long, responsible for the scar on his cheek.

Gameplay

Sho is a versatile character with plenty of strong cancelable moves and a divekick that works from any jump angle. His air attacks and dashes help to keep constant pressure, making it hard to escape when cornered.

Defense

Anti-air
Use 214K, close st.HP or cr.HP for anti-air. If Sho’s cr.HP is beaten by aerial moves, use 214K for its startup invincibility.

Almost all of Sho's normals have juggle properties and are special and super-cancelable, allowing more damage with conversions into 214K or any super.

Air-to-air
With meter, backjump LP and LK are great air options since you can cancel into air super on whiff or hit.

Breaker
Break with 214HK or backdash. both offer startup invincibility.

Offense

Jump LK
Jump LK has high priority over most normals and certain specials, stays active, and is super-cancelable. On whiff, you can even follow up with an air super.

In short, with meter, it strengthens your aerial game and makes opponents hesitant to anti-air.

Divekick
Use low-height divekicks for close-range pressure. With meter, you gain a strong high/low mixup using fuzzy jump or cr.LK.

⚠️ At high heights or on hit, divekicks can be vulnerable to the break mechanic, including throws, invincible reversals, and backdashes.

Movement
Cancel Sho's dash (run) with cr.LK or st.HP; it recovers quickly and is safe on block, making it great for closing distance and finding openings safely.

Combos
Sho has numerous combo possibilities thanks to the game's juggle properties and his mostly cancelable normals. Check the combos section for more info.

⚠️ You should avoid using st.HP in combos, it often results in breakable situations. If you're close enough end your combos with cr.HP > 214HK or cr.HK > Super.

Knee

Knee can be risky depending on range. You might end up in throw range or leave a punishable gap in the knee animation. However, when the knee hits on block, it seems even, allowing you to counter a throw attempt with 214HK.

Okizeme

When your opponent's on the ground, using a fireball into Run is a solid okizeme. You can then either do a run-stop and react, or simply cancel the run with an anti-air move.

Chip damage

To finish off your low-health opponent, use 464P or 236HK.
464P deals the most chip damage. You can try using it immediately after a divekick.

Fuzzy

Force a high block with a jump attack, then mix up with low or high before their recovery.

Sho excels with his super-cancelable jump HK into air Super, which is safe and deals significant damage.

Corner reset

Corner reset
Launch your opponent when he's in the corner with either cr.HP or close HP.HP followed by Knee (LK+HP), then execute a side switch :
  • walk under (condor)
  • dash empty (sho, rila, tia, saizo, baihu)
  • crouch dash attack (pielle, alsion, maherl, dao-long)

Side switch

Side switch
To keep out of the corner, use 236K as your combo ender.
ex: cr.LK, cr.LK > cr.HP > 236K

Saizo

Rasen Kyaku
Overhead, negative on hit and block with a long recovery. Leaves ample time for punishment. Saizo rarely resorts to this move unless finishing off his opponent

Ryuuei Kyaku
A slide that must be canceled to be safe. Slide into bomb is safe midscreen against Sho (cr.LK+HK > Hiryuu HP). At close range in the corner, punish slide > bomb with cr.LK.

Yashagami
Yashagami HP knocks down opponents, while Yashagami LP serves as a reversal and anti-air. This move must be canceled into a super due to its negative frames on hit and block. Be cautious on block; Saizo still has a few frames to execute a super, causing hesitation in your usual punishment. In such instances, Sho's Super (214214K) ensures a guaranteed punish

st.HP
Close st.HP hits low and is safe, while the far version has significant recovery, making it punishable even on hit.

Engetsuzan
punish with Run > st.HP

Pielle

Saber Bash
Despite its animation, this move isn't an overhead. Punish with cr.LK or Super (236236P) and on whiff with 214HK

Flying Peach Bomber
This one is an overhead, but if done close to you, it will whiff, and he'll pass behind you. When used in a blockstring, there's always a gap, no matter which normal move it was canceled from. Punish on whiff with 214HK

Bonjour Shot
A projectile with a very slow recovery. You can punish it from the other side of the screen with your Super (214214K). At close range, punish on block or hit with cr.LK or cr.HP.

Robin Liner
An anti-air special with slow recovery and no reversal properties... On hit, LP version keeps you standing for a punish, while HP version knocks you down.

Monsieur Crash
A three-hit move, easily punishable. However, Pielle can delay each hit to counter your punish attempt.

Merci Un Deux Trois
An aerial dive that can deceive your anti-air or jump. When you block all 3 hits, Pielle passes behind you. Punish with cr.LK

Super Rose
A super that crosses the entire screen with a wide hitbox, useful for countering jump attempts. Whiff punish at super flash with 214HK.

Thunder Croiser

Sho

Onigawara
An overhead, safe on block, and cancelable. it's punishable during its startup frames. It's also breakable when canceled into 236HK.

Tobihizageri
Safe on block, but depending on the range, He might end up within your throw range or leave an opening in the knee animation where you can react with 214K.

Sashikomi Kick
At high height and if Sho has no meter, you can reliably counter it with your anti-air (cr.HP, st.LP, 214HK). It is also breakable with 214HK, backdash, or throw (though throw can be countered by 214HK)

Rekkou Ha
Punish at point-blank range. Sho's Super 214214K goes through fireballs (from backdash range) and can punish blockstrings like cr.LK x 3 > cr.HP x 236P.

Ranki Ryuu Geri
Ranki Ryuu Geri is tricky to punish as it can be followed by air super. However, Ranki Ryuu Geri is minus on block. If you have meter, a forward j.HK > SUPER guarantees a punish.

Geki Rekkou Ha
Break with backdash if used at a high height.

  • divekick ❌ air super
  • fuzzy j.HK ❌ air super

Maherl

Abdomen Step
This short step is punishable with cr.LK if he is very close to you. It's safe if it was canceled by st.LK (st.LK creates a slight pushback). However, you can always break or whiff punish with 214HK

Slash Sword
Can reach you from very far away. Safe at maximum range. At mid and close range, punish with st.HP.

Punish Sword
Travels a shorter distance than Slash Words. It is also Safe at maximum range. At close range, punish with st.HP.

Fire Breath
Punish LP version on block with j.HK and on whiff with super (236236P). For HP version, you can interrupt with any normals immediately after blocking the first or second hit.

Balloon Bomb
Overhead, whiff punish with st.HP/cr.HP or 214HK. on block, punish with run > st.HP

Cadabra Bomber
A Super that can go through projectiles up close. Punish with any Super or run > st.HP

Shiva Dance
A Super with invincible startup that is safe on block. Punishable up close with a backdash after blocking first hit.

Tia

Normals
All her normals are safe on block. Tia pressures Sho well with her dash attack, especially dash > st.HK, doubling as a powerful anti-air and setup for a super juggle. To stop her dash attacks, Sho needs to quickly and preemptively input cr.LK.

Soul Spike

Ground Soul Spike LK/HK are not overhead, block low.
Soul Spike LK has pushback and minimal recovery. HK version has no pushback, making it easier to with st.HP or Throw.

Before getting hit in combos, you can break both versions with a throw, backdash, or super

Check her super meter before punishing. Tia may counter with Burning Revolver. Throw punishes Soul Spike HK

Mirage Spear Kick

Rising upper
Rising Upper LP is an anti-air reversal with quick recovery, making it difficult to punish on whiff. It's invulnerability on startup allows her to pass through projectiles. When blocked at close range, punish with st.HP.

Rapid Gale
Her fireball has a slightly slow recovery. She mostly uses it for okizeme or pressure. You can whiff punish with Super (214214K) at backdash range.

Heel Break
2-hit sequence, last hit is an overhead. After the first hit, punish or break with throw, 214HK or super.

Rapid Charge
She switches sides mid-air on block, quickly turn your character to punish.

Burning Revolver
Punish with dash > st.HP

Cutlass Flasher
Overhead super, switches sides on block.

Alsion III

Horus Shade
An overhead that is safe but has a slow startup, making it easily reactable. Punish on whiff with 214HK or cr.HP

Death Shred
This one is also an overhead with a slow startup but has a larger pushback. It's a two-hitter when the opponent is standing. Second hit is overhead. If crouched, whiff punish with cr.LK; standing, interrupt before 2nd hit with cr.LK.

Poison Fog
Close-range projectile for chip damage or pressure on your wakeup.

Deadly Claw
When executed close to you, the LK version lands in the same spot, while the HK version lands behind you. Punish at close range with Super (236236P) and on whiff with 214HK.

Dash Knee
A special dash attack that must be executed at the right spacing to be safe. Punish on block or hit with cr.LK

Anubis Hug
Super projectile with invul. startup. Punish at close range with cr.LK

Pharaoh Dance
Super projectile with invul. startup. If you block it, he retreats very far. Punish with Super (214214K) or run > st.HP
