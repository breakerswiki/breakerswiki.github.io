Sho Kamui

History

Sho Kamui, Breakers Revenge's Karate master and protagonist, joins the FIST Tournament to showcase his martial art's power and confront his old rival, Lee Dao-Long, responsible for the scar on his cheek.

Gameplay

Sho's got plenty of offensive moves you can cancel, along with strong specials, a safe air fireball super, and a divekick that works from any jump direction.

His main strength lies in maintaining pressure through his jumps, divekicks, and dashes. Once he has cornered you, escaping becomes challenging.

Defense

Use 214HK or cr.HP into 214HK for anti-air. Generally, cr.HP works best in most matchups.

Break with 214HK or backdash to dodge. Both have startup invincibility. Read more in "Matchups" section.

Offense

Get in on the aerial game; his jump LK can cancel into a Super and works great against air-to-air normals and some specials. Using divekicks in pressure to open your opponent's defense. Note: At a high height, divekicks can be countered with throw, invincible reversal, or backdash.

Quick dashes with cr.LK or st.HP recover fast, great for getting close and finding openings safely.

Finish combos with either 236HK or 236LK to switch sides and keep out of the corner.

Don't overuse st.HP in combos, it often results in breakable situations. If you're close enough, use cr.HP or cr.HK instead.

Knee

Using that move can get risky, you might end up in throw range or leave a gap in the knee animation that the opponent can punish.
cr.LK, cr.LK > cr.HP > LK+HP : throw range
cr.LK, cr.LP, cr.LK > cr.HP > LK+HP : whiff punish

Okizeme

When your opponent's on the ground, using a fireball into Run is a solid okizeme. You can then either do a run-stop and react, or simply cancel the run with an anti-air move.

Chip damage

To finish off your low-health opponent, use 464P or 236HK. 464P deals the most chip damage. You can try using it immediately after a divekick. See more ...

Fuzzy

Trigger your opponent's guard with a jump attack that forces them to block high, then before they recover from blockstun, either go low or high again. Great tool for Sho with his super-cancelable jump HK or crouch HK, dealing big damage often. See more ...

Corner Resets

Launch your opponent when he's in the corner with either cr.HP or close HP.HP followed by Knee (LK+HP), then execute a side switch :
  1. walk under (condor)
  2. dash empty (sho, rila, tia, saizo, baihu)
  3. crouch dash attack (pielle, alsion, maherl, dao-long)
